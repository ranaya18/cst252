How to make pasta.

  1. Boil a pot of water at medium high temp.
  2. Put a pinch of salt into the boiling water.
  3. Use a box of Penne pasta.
  4. Let it boil for 10 minuets.
  5. While the pasta is boiling, make the tamato sauce
  6. In a saute pan add oil, one cut of garlic, and dices of onion. When the garlic and onion brown, use a whole can of crushed tomato. Then add the whole can of tomato sauce. When that starts to boil, add a half can of tomato paste. Start to add a little bit of salt, Italiana spice, Oregino, and sugar to the sauce.
  7. Let the sauce sit for 2 minutes. While it's sitting, check on the pasta.
  8. After straining the pasta of any excess water. Enjoy a bowl of Penne patsa with homemade tomato sauce
